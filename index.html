About

I am a Research Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), China since 2021. Prior to that, I was a Post-Doctoral Fellow of Computer Science and Technology at SUSTech during 2019 and 2021(PI: Prof. Yuhui Shi).

I obtained a Ph.D. degree in Management Science and Engineering from Beijing University of Technology, China in 2019 and was a Joint Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of New South Wales, Australia from 2017 to 2018 (Supervisor: Prof. Ruhul Sarker). Here is my CV.

My research interests include:
   Evolutionary Computation and Optimization
   Automated Algorithm Design
   Operations Research

Some of my research findings have been successfully applied in industry. For example, for the supply chain management of a manufacturing company, our techniques are improving the efficiency of row material stacking, saving spaces for rack placement, and automatically configuring solvers for demand changes.
