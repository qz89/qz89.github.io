About

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology, China (PI: Prof. Yuhui Shi).

Before that, I obtained the Ph.D. degree in management science and engineering from Beijing University of Technology (BJUT), China, in 2019 and was a joint Ph.D. student at the University of New South Wales, Australia (funded by China Scholarship Council) during 2017 to 2018 (Supervisor: Prof. Ruhul Sarker).

My research interests include:
   Evolutionary optimization, in particular, multi-objective optimization and dynamic optimization
   Evolutionary learning, specific in evolutionary clustering
   Applications of evolutionary optimization/learning to finance, risk analysis, etc
   Automated evolutionary algorithm design
